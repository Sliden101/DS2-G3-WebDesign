Our Story

Founded in 2015, BookHaven began as a passion project by a group of book lovers who believed everyone deserves access to quality literature at affordable prices.

What started as a small local bookshop has grown into a thriving online community of readers from around the world. Today, we curate over 50,000 titles across all genres and deliver them to thousands of happy readers every month.

Our mission remains simple: to make great books accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone.
